Dieter Rams Good Design
10
Principles
of Design
Linh Huynh University of Illinois-Chicago
1

Good design is innovative.

Just like technology is always innovating, so should design.

3

Good design is aesthetic.

The most satisfying aspect of design, because we love it when something looks good.

5

Good design is unobtrusive

It is essential for design to be subjective, but design must also leave room for one's own interpretation.

7

Good design is long-lasting.

Design that transcends time and never goes out of style.

9

Good design is environmentally-friendly.

Design should contribute in bettering the community, however, product production may cost a few trees.

2

Good design makes a product useful.

There is always a meaning or purpose in what we make.

4

Good design makes a product understandable.

If someone can't figure out the product's function, we made it too complex.

6

Good design is honest.

We design to convey a message of self-expression. Not to manipulate.

8

Good design is through down to the last detail.

Designers must consider as many different groups of people as possible.

10

Good design is as little design as possible.

To the point and doesn't over saturate.
